Her core competency is ensuring the smooth functioning of various aspects of Human Resources Management within the organization. This includes recruiting and retaining talented Faculty and administrative staff, ensuring compliance with employment laws and regulations, developing and implementing HR policies and procedures, handling employee relations and performance management, managing compensation and benefits programs through her vast experience of over 15 years in People Management and endless dedication.

Her role is pivotal in supporting the Chairman & CEO in their vision and working closely with the Core Team of Patshala and ensuring it is fostering a positive work culture across all teams.

Apart from supporting Patshala and her own family of 2 kids backed by a very supportive life partner, she efficiently leads the team’s for American based IT firm Tulasi RnD Techies Incorporation and International women lingerie store Only TWAM & AI driven VFX Firm Infinite Illusions and is fondly called as an “Super Woman” across all the organizations being a behemoth as needed in daily leadership & strategic operations.
